Shipping Policy

At ElectronicsOnly, we strive to deliver your orders quickly and reliably. This Shipping Policy explains how and when your order will be processed and delivered.

Order Processing

Orders are processed Monday through Friday, excluding major holidays. Most orders are shipped within 1–3 business days after payment confirmation.

Shipping Locations

Worldwide shipping is available. All orders are charged in U.S. dollars (USD). Free shipping is available for United States orders only when shown at checkout; international customers are responsible for shipping costs to their delivery country or location.

Shipping Costs

  • Free shipping applies to United States orders only when selected at checkout.
  • Shipping costs apply based on the order size, shipping speed, and delivery destination.
  • International standard shipping is usually about 7–14 business days, expedited shipping about 4–7 business days, and express shipping about 2–5 business days, depending on the country and carrier.
  • For international orders, customers are responsible for any destination shipping charges, customs duties, taxes, or import fees required by their country.
  • All product prices and checkout totals are shown and paid in U.S. dollars (USD).

Tracking Information

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive tracking information via email so you can monitor the delivery status of your package.

Delivery Time

Delivery times may vary depending on your location and the shipping carrier. United States free shipping is typically 3–5 business days after shipment. International delivery times vary by destination: standard shipping is usually about 7–14 business days, expedited shipping about 4–7 business days, and express shipping about 2–5 business days. Customs or carrier delays may extend delivery times.

Shipping Delays

While we do our best to ensure timely delivery, ElectronicsOnly is not responsible for delays caused by shipping carriers, weather conditions, or other circumstances beyond our control.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about your shipment, please contact us through our Contact Page.
