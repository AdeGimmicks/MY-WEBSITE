Shipping Policy

At ElectronicsOnly, we strive to deliver your orders quickly and reliably. This Shipping Policy explains how and when your order will be processed and delivered.

Order Processing

Orders are processed Monday through Friday, excluding major holidays. Most orders are shipped within 1–3 business days after payment confirmation.

Shipping Locations

Currently, we ship within the United States only. International shipping is not available at this time.

Shipping Costs

  • Shipping costs may apply depending on the order size and delivery location.
  • The exact shipping fee will be calculated and displayed at checkout before payment.

Tracking Information

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive tracking information via email so you can monitor the delivery status of your package.

Delivery Time

Delivery times may vary depending on your location and the shipping carrier. Typical delivery time is between 3–7 business days after shipment.

Shipping Delays

While we do our best to ensure timely delivery, ElectronicsOnly is not responsible for delays caused by shipping carriers, weather conditions, or other circumstances beyond our control.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about your shipment, please contact us through our Contact Page.
