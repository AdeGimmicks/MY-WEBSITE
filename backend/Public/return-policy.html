Return & Refund Policy

At ElectronicsOnly, customer satisfaction is important to us. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return the item under the conditions outlined below.

Return Period

  • Returns must be requested within 14 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused and returned in their original packaging.
  • Products showing signs of use or damage may not be eligible for a refund.

Return Shipping

  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs.
  • ElectronicsOnly does not cover return shipping fees unless the item received is defective or incorrect.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

  • If approved, the refund will be issued to your original payment method.
  • Please allow several business days for the refund to appear in your account depending on your payment provider.

Contact Us

Before returning any product, please contact us first through our Contact Page so we can approve and guide the return process.
